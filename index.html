Photo Gallery

This is a Photo Gallery app that allows you to manage your photos with ease. With a simple swipe, you can decide whether to keep or delete photos from your device. The app provides a user-friendly interface to review your photos in a card-like format. Swipe right to keep the photo and move to the next one, or swipe left to delete the photo from your storage, freeing up space. The app also provides a progress indicator showing how many photos you've reviewed. Enjoy a clutter-free photo gallery with our app!
